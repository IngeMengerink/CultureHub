The background story of Culture Hubs

Culture Hubs has been established as a result of the project ‘’Virtual Product Development’’. During a period of nine weeks we, five Industrial Design Engineering students from the University of Twente, were busy designing a product service system (PSS). The goal of this project was to create a combination of a physical product and a service, such that the product would not work properly without the service and vice versa.

Next to that, all of us followed diverse tools during the period. We could use the gained insights from these eleven tools to make our PSS more suitable for the market. Also, some of the tools helped us to create nice visualisations of concepts or the PSS itself. These are integrated in this website to carry out our idea.
