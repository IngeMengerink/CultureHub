Welcome to Culture Hubs

Start exploring the city in a new way

Culture Hubs is a product service system which will guide you through various cities. By following a route in the app you will enter a story about a specific city-relevant topic. Along the route you will visit hubs and AR stickers which will bring the story even more to life by using Augmented Reality, movement, lights and more.

Also, for the younger ones a tour with Culture Hubs will be an unforgettable experience. Characters their own age will guide them through the city by talking about their lives and playing little games in between.

Wanting to give your city a boost? Wanting to attract more tourists and customers? Well, Culture Hubs may be just the thing you need. Take a look at the diverse topics on our site for more information. Be smart, give your city a boost, invest in Culture Hubs.
